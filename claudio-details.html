Claudio Di Sipio

Claudio Di Sipio is a postdoctoral researcher in the Department of Engineering, Mathematics, and Computer Science at the University of L'Aquila. He took his Ph.D in 2023 at the University of L'Aquila, and he has been an invited researcher at GEODES lab, University of Montreal, for six months. His research interests include recommendation systems for software engineering, mining OSS repositories, model-driven engineering, and application of ML/AI techniques for software engineering. He served as a program committee member for different international conferences, including ASE, MSR, FORGE, and MODELS. In addition, he served as a reviewer for the IEEE Transactions on Software Engineering (TSE), the Springer Software and Systems Modeling (SoSyM), and Knowledge and Information Systems journals. Further information about him is available at https://claudiodsi.github.io/
